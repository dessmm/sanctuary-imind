# Site Vision: Sanctuary — iMind Psychological Services

## 1. Project Overview
**Sanctuary** is the digital home of iMind Psychological Services, a licensed psychology clinic in Dumaguete, Philippines. The site bridges the gap between professional mental healthcare and the people in Negros Oriental who need it.

**Core Goal:** Convert visitors into booked appointments through a warm, trustworthy, and friction-free experience.

## 2. Target Audience
- Adults 25–55 seeking individual therapy or counseling
- Parents seeking assessments for children
- Professionals experiencing burnout or anxiety
- Students dealing with academic/social stress

## 3. Brand Voice
- **Tone:** Calm, professional, empathetic — never clinical or cold
- **Key messages:** "You are safe here", "Evidence-based care", "Real people, real results"
